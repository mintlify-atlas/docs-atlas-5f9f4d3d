## What is Recipes & Food Manager?

Recipes & Food Manager is a Python application that helps you take control of what you eat and what you spend on groceries. You define **Food** items — each with a price per kilogram, energy in kcal per 100 g, and optional nutritional data — and then combine them into **Recipes** that track ingredients, quantities, and preparation time.

The app persists everything to local JSON files, so your data stays on your machine with no account or internet connection required.

## How the data flows

```
settings.yaml  ──►  SETTINGS constant
                         │
                         ▼
     Food objects  +  Recipe objects
           │               │
           └──────┬────────┘
                  ▼
          JSON data files
                  │
                  ▼
          Statistics & reports
```

The app loads your food and recipe data from JSON files at startup, lets you create or modify objects in memory, and then saves everything back to disk when you are done.
